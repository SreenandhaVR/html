Introduction of Video and Audio Elements

Introduction of audio and video elements was a big step forward in the development of HTML.The ability to easily embed video and audio into web pages was a game-changer and replaced older plugin based solutions like Flash and Silverlight,helping to drive the growth of the streaming media online with platforms like YouTube and SoundCloud as some of the early adopters.

Supported Video Formats

  • MP4-Supported across all major browers and offers good compression and quality.
  • WebM is a open source format that delivers high quality video and is optimized for web use.
  • Ogg is an old open source format less commonly used and that is still a viable option in some cases.

    Captions,Subtitles and Chapters

    captions are transcripts of the audio content.They are necessary for people with learning impairments and helpful for various situations like a noisy coffee shop or uite situations like libraries where playing audio would distrub others.Subtitles are translated versions of the audio content.Chapters are markers that divide the content into sections allowing users to navigate through different parts of the media.
