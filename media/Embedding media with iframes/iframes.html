Embedding media with iframes

If you've ever used the embed option on Youtube,Spotify or sme other media platform to add a video,song, or playlist to a webpage,you've likely encountered the iframe element.It's an effective way to display content from another source.With iframe we can embed content from one webpage into another webpage.

Basic iframe Syntax

    <iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Dd1ZJiCveGY" 
     width="560" height="315" title="Youtube Video Player"</iframe>
